# M2E Multichannel Connect for Magento 1

M2E Multichannel Connect is a trusted Magento 1 extension that allows you to expand your online business beyond your store by connecting it with top marketplaces like Shein, Temu, TikTok Shop, OnBuy, Kaufland, and Otto.

With this all-in-one extension, you can list your Magento products on multiple sales channels, manage orders, and keep inventory synchronized within your Magento 1 admin panel.
